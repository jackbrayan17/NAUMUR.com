Discover our areas of expertise to turn your projects into tangible results.

Expertise

  • Inclusive Finance
    We transform access to financial services into a driver of economic growth and social equity. Naumur supports you in designing and implementing innovative financial strategies and products targeting traditionally excluded populations and micro-enterprises. Leveraging digital technologies, we help you develop solutions that contribute to economic empowerment—especially for women—and resilience to climate shocks. We make inclusion a driver of sustainable development.
  • Public Finance
    We support public institutions and administrations in modernizing their financial management. Our expertise covers the development of transparency and budgetary accountability strategies, tax reform, optimization of public spending, and capacity building. We help design and implement robust reforms that improve public action efficiency, restore citizen trust, and create an investment-friendly environment. Benefit from high-level advisory for public finance management in service of national development.
  • Accounting and Taxation
    As an approved management center (CGA), Naumur is your strategic partner for smooth and high-performing financial and tax management. Whether you are an established company or an entrepreneur, our experts assist you on all accounting, tax, legal, and social matters. Beyond mere regulatory compliance, we advise you to optimize your structure, secure your growth, and maximize your performance. We put our technical expertise and proximity at the service of your long-term success.
  • Business Support
    Your ambition, our roadmap. We support companies at every critical stage of their development. Our pragmatic approach combines in-depth strategic analysis with operational implementation alongside your teams. We intervene in defining your vision, optimizing your organizational model, improving processes, and managing change. We help turn your challenges into real drivers of competitiveness and value.
  • Financial Advisory and Engineering
    We bring cutting-edge expertise in financial and strategic engineering. We assist you in structuring large-scale projects, securing funding, managing mergers and acquisitions, and designing innovative investment vehicles. Focused on capital markets, our intervention covers the entire value chain—from initial strategic planning to final negotiation—ensuring rigorous process management for optimal results.
  • Monitoring and Evaluation
    Measure to better steer impact. In a world where accountability is crucial, we provide robust monitoring and evaluation (M&E) systems for development projects and public policies. We design tailored performance measurement frameworks based on key indicators, enabling progress tracking, real impact assessment, and real-time strategy adjustment. Our solutions ensure that every investment—public or private—delivers tangible returns and social impact.
  • Entrepreneurship
    We believe entrepreneurs are the architects of the economic future. This hub is dedicated to strengthening entrepreneurial ecosystems and providing direct support to SMEs. We operate on two levers: supporting public authorities in designing startup-friendly policies and environments; and directly advising entrepreneurs on growth, access to financing, and consolidation amid sector funding gaps. From idea to scale-up, we are the partner of builders.
  • Big Data, Digital & Automation
    Empower your decision-making through the data revolution. We help you capture, analyze, and leverage your data to generate actionable insights. Our business intelligence, process digitalization, and intelligent automation solutions transform how you operate. Whether modernizing a public administration, optimizing a supply chain, or developing new digital services, we put technology at the service of your performance, innovation, and competitive advantage.
  • Economic Studies
    Intelligence that guides decision-making in the face of complex contemporary economic challenges—we transform data into true strategic compasses. Our economic studies unit offers you a deep and nuanced understanding of market dynamics, sectoral trends, and growth opportunities. We firmly believe that rigorous and forward-looking analyses are the essential foundation of any winning strategy. At NAUMUR, we make economic intelligence a concrete lever for sustainable development and performance.
